  • Node, Twitter API, & NLP_Compromise
  • Algorithms used:
  • Fisher-Yates

EC Content Management Tool

EC Content Management Tool is a front-facing API that scrapes competitor sites like Craigslist, Recycler, Locanto, and Mascus, and converts the listings to CSV format and uploaded to El Clasificado's database.

  • Technologies Used:
  • Rails, jQuery, Selenium & Mechanize
  • Github Repo: EC Content Management Tool

Map My Snaps

Map My Snaps is the app Machiko and I won fourth place for at Hack for LA. It displays every location that accepts EBT and WIC in the Boyle Heights area.

Pool Party
